Javascript & the DOM

An analogy to describe JavaScript and its relationship to HTML and CSS

CSS is the building plans, HTML is the building, Javascript is the wiring, and you are the architect, the builder, the electrician, and much more.

Without the wiring linking everything up, what good is that fancy new house? Your key won't open that flash new electronic front door lock, your TV won't be beaming The Simpsons into your living room, and a snap of your fingers won't dim the lights and fade in the soothing sounds of Barry White.
